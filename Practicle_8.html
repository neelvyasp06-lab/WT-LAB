Vision

To impart quality technical education through research, innovation and teamwork to create professionally superior and ethically strong manpower.

  • Prepare graduates for lifelong learning and ethical challenges.

  • PSO2: Tackle future technological challenges in areas like Security, AI, IoT, etc.
